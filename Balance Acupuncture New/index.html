Our Treatments

Acupuncture & Herbal Medicine

We offer two complementary pathways to healing — used individually or together for the most complete therapeutic effect. Both are rooted in Traditional Chinese Medicine and adapted to your unique needs.

Acupuncture

Ancient wisdom, modern care

Acupuncture works by stimulating specific points along the body's energy pathways, encouraging your body's natural ability to heal, regulate and restore balance.

Fine, single-use needles are placed with precision and care. Most people find the experience deeply relaxing — often drifting into a restorative rest during their session.

From chronic pain and hormonal imbalance to anxiety and digestive health, acupuncture offers a gentle yet profound approach to long-term wellbeing.

Acupuncture Treatments
Chinese Herbal Medicine

Personalised prescriptions from nature

Chinese herbal medicine is one of the oldest and most sophisticated systems of natural medicine in the world. Working alongside your acupuncture treatment, herbal formulas extend and deepen your therapeutic care.

Each prescription is carefully tailored to your individual pattern of health — not just your symptoms. Using time-tested combinations of roots, leaves, flowers and bark, your formula evolves as you do.

Safe, personalised and rooted in a tradition spanning thousands of years, herbal medicine offers powerful support for the body's natural healing processes.

Meet Sue

A practitioner who listens

Sue qualified from the renowned Northern College of Acupuncture with a first-class BSc (Hons) degree and is a fully registered member of the British Acupuncture Council (BAcC).

Her personal journey with Chinese medicine — first as a patient, then as a practitioner — has shaped a deeply empathetic approach to care. She combines acupuncture with Chinese herbal medicine, bodywork, Guasha, cupping and tailored lifestyle guidance.

Sue believes that good health is always possible, and that the role of a practitioner is to listen, interpret and guide — never to impose a one-size-fits-all solution.
