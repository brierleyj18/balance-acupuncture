What is it?

Rooted in nature, tailored to you

Chinese herbal medicine is one of the oldest continuous medical traditions in the world, with a documented history spanning over 2,500 years. It forms one of the core pillars of Traditional Chinese Medicine, alongside acupuncture.

Herbal formulas are composed of carefully selected combinations of natural plant materials — roots, bark, leaves, seeds and flowers — each with distinct therapeutic properties, combined synergistically to address your specific pattern of health.

Unlike supplements or single-herb remedies, a Chinese herbal prescription treats the whole person. Two patients with the same Western diagnosis may receive entirely different formulas — because Chinese medicine looks beneath the symptom to understand the underlying imbalance.

At Balance Acupuncture, herbal medicine is offered both alongside acupuncture and as a standalone therapeutic option.

The integrated approach

How herbal medicine supports acupuncture

Acupuncture and Chinese herbal medicine are the two primary branches of Traditional Chinese Medicine — designed to work in concert for a depth of care that neither achieves alone.

Continuity

Acupuncture works in the session. Herbal medicine works between sessions — maintaining and extending the therapeutic momentum so your body continues to heal around the clock.

Depth

Where acupuncture is powerful for moving Qi and addressing structural imbalances, herbal medicine excels at nourishing deep constitutional weaknesses that require sustained internal support.

Precision

Your herbal prescription is reviewed and refined at every appointment. As your health picture changes, your formula evolves with it — always precisely matched to where you are right now.
