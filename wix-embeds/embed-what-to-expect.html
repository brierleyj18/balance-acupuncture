Your appointment

What to expect

Every visit to Balance Acupuncture follows the same attentive, unhurried approach. Here is how a typical session unfolds.

01

Your consultation

Your first appointment is in-depth. We discuss your main health concern alongside a wide range of questions about your current and historical health, lifestyle and wellbeing — taking the time to truly understand you.

02

Your treatment

Following your consultation, fine acupuncture needles are placed with care at your specific prescription of points. Many people find the experience deeply relaxing — some drift gently off to sleep.

03

Your herbal prescription

Where appropriate, Sue will prepare a personalised Chinese herbal formula to support your treatment between sessions. Your prescription evolves alongside your acupuncture as your health improves.

04

Session length

Appointments last between 40 and 50 minutes. Please wear loose, comfortable clothing and ensure you have eaten something before your visit. Avoid heavy meals or alcohol immediately beforehand.

05

Your treatment plan

After your initial appointment, Sue will outline an estimated treatment plan tailored to your condition. For chronic conditions, weekly sessions are typically recommended for a period of time to achieve lasting results.

06

Needles & sensation

Acupuncture needles are extremely fine — as thin as 0.15mm. Most people feel very little on insertion. Any sensation is typically a mild ache or tingling. Once in place, the needles are comfortable and you rest for around 20 minutes.
