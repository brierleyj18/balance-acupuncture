The process

Your herbal medicine journey

From your first conversation to your evolving prescription, here is how herbal medicine works at Balance Acupuncture.

01

Your consultation

Sue takes a thorough case history, exploring not only your presenting symptoms but your overall constitution — digestion, sleep, energy, emotional state and more. This holistic picture forms the foundation of your prescription.

02

Your diagnosis

Using tongue and pulse assessment alongside detailed questioning, Sue identifies your underlying pattern of disharmony in Chinese medicine terms. This is what your prescription is built around — not just your symptoms.

03

Your formula

Your personalised herbal formula is carefully prepared using quality-tested, ethically sourced herbs. Sue will explain what your formula is designed to address and answer any questions about the ingredients.

04

Ongoing review

Your prescription is reassessed at every appointment and adjusted as your health evolves. Herbal medicine is dynamic — your formula should change as you do, always meeting you where you are.
