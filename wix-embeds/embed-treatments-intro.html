Acupuncture & Herbal Medicine

Two complementary pathways to healing — used individually or together for the most complete therapeutic effect. Both rooted in Traditional Chinese Medicine and adapted entirely to your needs.

Acupuncture

Ancient wisdom, modern care

Acupuncture works by stimulating specific points along the body's energy pathways, encouraging your natural ability to heal, regulate and restore balance.

Fine, single-use needles are placed with precision and care. Most people find the experience deeply relaxing — often drifting into a restorative rest during their session.

From chronic pain and hormonal imbalance to anxiety and digestive health, acupuncture offers a gentle yet profound approach to long-term wellbeing.

Better together with Herbal Medicine

Many patients find that combining acupuncture with a personalised herbal prescription deepens and extends the therapeutic benefits between sessions.

Chinese Herbal Medicine

Personalised prescriptions from nature

Chinese herbal medicine is one of the oldest and most sophisticated systems of natural medicine in the world — with a continuous clinical history spanning over 2,500 years.

Each prescription is carefully tailored to your individual pattern of health. Using time-tested combinations of roots, leaves, flowers and bark, your formula evolves as you do — working with your body's natural healing processes.

Offered alongside acupuncture or as a standalone therapy, herbal medicine extends and deepens your therapeutic care between sessions.
